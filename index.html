Algorithm: http://codereview.stackexchange.com/questions/40189/pig-latin-translator-in-ruby-and-rspec

  • Yoda speaks short sentences. 'Hello' will just be repeated.

  • Use proper English, grammar, and punctuation. For example: Don't instead of Dont.

  • Use extra punctuation if a sentence contains for than one clause split with 'and', 'or', etc. For example: "Put your hands up and step away from turnip." should be "Put your hands up, and step away from the turnip."

  • Paranthesis are not recommended.
